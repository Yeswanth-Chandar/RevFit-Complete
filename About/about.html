The Project

RevFit as a idea came to us after dealing with lots of fake reviews, When you try to find something useful those fake reviews make it more difficult to have a clear Decision.

So we decided to make the web as free of fake reviews as possible and to do that we needed a automation tool to do it. And thats how we created RevFit. A Powerful Tool to Filter Reviews And make your browsing experience better.

Our Goal

Our Goal is to Chop off as many unwanted reviews as possible, By improving our tool to get good at Filtering & blocking. And to give people freedom of choice by presenting clean reviews.
