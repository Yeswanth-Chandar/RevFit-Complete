What is RevFit

RevFit is a free extension that allows you to customize your web experience. You can Filter Reviews, Block Fake Reviews and lots more. It’s available for all major desktop browsers and for your mobile devices.

RevFit is an open source project which can be access by anyone who is interested in it, It is Free to use and will be free forever.

How does RevFit work?

Choose what you want to see when browsing the web by using filter lists to block unwanted Reviews, like Bots or Fake human Reviews.

What Reviews can be Filtered

Filter lists are sets of rules that tell your browser which elements to block. You can block as little or as much as you want. Choose from pre-made, externally maintained filter lists, or create your own (English only). Almost all pre-made filter lists are created, published and maintained by users for users under open source development.

Many reviews in popular websites can be done by bots. Because of this, RevFit provides certain levels of Blocking and Filtering by default. If you want, you can add additional Blocking and filter lists to increase your filter rate.

Default filter lists

The following filter lists are enabled by default when you install RevFit:

Automatic Filter

A filter list that blocks Bot reviews (e.g. ML emotion engine) based on your browser’s language settings.

Revfit will automatically try to identify and filter reviews based on the infomation provided by AI

Selectable Filter

A feature that supports a better web by allowing nonintrusive, free to filter options. Using this user can select if they want that review or not .

This list is open source. anyone can improve, modify, suggest imporvments.

These filter lists are enabled to get you started. You can keep them, modify them, remove them and / or add other filter lists - it’s up to you.

Privacy Conerns ?

We do not specifically collect any user data. Most of your data (e.g. what websites you visit) is never sent to our servers to begin with. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

How can you help RevFit grow

Our main source of revenue comes as part of Donations, which is managed by everyone of our co-developers.

Implementing the Donatable criteria is part of RevFit attempt to pursue a different vision of review Filtering. It requires significant effort on RevFit’s side including, but not limited to, ongoing review and monitoring of the individal, Filtering participants, and providing technical support. These services cannot be done as a hobby, or completely taken over by volunteers.

Therefore we only charge large entities a license fee so that we can offer the same Review services to everyone and maintain our resources to develop the best software for our users.

NOTE: Around 90 percent of licenses are granted for free to smaller entities.

Since our software is not paid and only small amount of revenue is gained from Business patners. We rely on donations to maintain our codes

Every entity on the allowlist - paying participant or non-paying participant - MUST maintain the same strict criteria that our users accepted as part of Filtering Reviews. We will never accept any ad that doesn't meet the criteria, and there is no way to buy a spot on the Review Filter list. Feel free to discuss and share your opinions.
